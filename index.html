Roadmap
Building a foundation
ATOR is ultimately built to be decentralized. But we are currently in active development, paralellizing hardware, protocol, community outreach, education and long-term planning. We have moved with breathtaking pace, and we are only accelerating.
Phase 1: ATOR Launch
  • Q1 2023 - ATOR ERC20 Token launched, with team present at ETH Denver. v1 Technical whitepaper released.
  • Q2 2023 - Gasless relay registration established and consensus weight metric finalized.
  • Q2 2023 - Relay Education series launched across written and video format.
Phase 2: Protocol and Relay Beta
  • Q1 2023 - Scope out the ATOR Relay, a low-powered device, through PCB and enclosure CAD mockups
  • Q2 2023 - ATOR Relay enclosure v1 prototype and BOM
  • Q3 2023 - ATOR Protocol enters Goerli Testnet
Phase 3: Hardware and Protocol Release
  • Q4 2023 - Protocol testing and scalability. Improved claims flow, dashboard and backend services
  • Q4 2023 / Q1 2024 - First preorder of ATOR Relay Hardware / Distribution
  • Q1/Q2 2024 - ATOR Protocol goes live on ETH Mainnet
Phase 4: Wider Scope and Networks
  • Q2 2024 - Additional, detailed incentivization structure for different relay types, setups and geographies
  • Q2/Q3 2024 - Full-scale rollout of the ATOR Relay Hardware, introduction of routing functionality
  • Q2 2024 - ATOR begin to release their own services on Tor
Phase 5: An Ecosystem of Services
  • Q3 2024 - Introduction of ATOR Governance, with a DAO, Protocol fund and decision-scope
  • Q3 2024 - Build out of beta ATOR third-party pools, for additional networks, funded via governance
  • Q4 2024 - Expansion to other decentralized and anonymity networks
